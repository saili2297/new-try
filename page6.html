Why Surveillance?

Surveillance is the continuous art of watching someone, someplace, or some object in order to document and identify any contacts, activities and whereabouts. It is generally conducted to prevent a crime, to obtain evidence of a crime, to obtain evidence of wrongful act, to document an individual’s location, movements, to pin-point activities at a location, to obtain any information or intelligence for a futuristic eventuality etc.
